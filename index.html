Feeding Hungry People Program


Hunger is biggest problem across the globe. As per World Health Organisation we produce food that is enough to feed double the population of the world, but still around 20 million people don't have enough food to feed themselves.

As a part of this program we collect unspoiled foods from school, universities,campuses and distribute to the people in need.

This Feeding Hungry People program was intiated in 2010 in support of fight against hunger. And today we have 1000+ volunteers present across 10+ cities in India, and has served 5 million people in need.

Money Donation Program


Zindagi RubRu Foundation is also works for Education, Health & Nutrition for Girl Child, Other than this we work for early symptoms & Other Causes that are responsible for Heart Disease, Cancer & Other major diseases.
